  1. Make predictions about a cricket match, and get points on the basis of how close your prediction was. Simple :-)
  2. For an exact prediction in each category you get 1000 points. Oh yes, a full 1-0-0-0 points!!
  3. If there is a difference between your predictions and actual events, your score decreases as per below rules -
    1. Player runs difference = R, then (your points) = 1000 - (R x 10)
    2. Player sixes difference = S, then (your points) = 1000 - (S x 100)
    3. Player wikcets difference = W, then (your points) = 1000 - (W x 100)
    4. Team runs difference = TR, then (your points) = 1000 - (TR x 5)
    5. Team sixes difference = TS, then (your points) = 1000 - (TS x 20)
  4. Thats it! Share your cricket gyan and score as many points as you can .. AND .. get ready to be surprised with some interesting prizes soon :-)
